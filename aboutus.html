ABOUT US

Before the pandemic, Peter Polillo (Owner) was a filmmaker/Video editor. Money is not hard for their family, all of their payments and bills are easily paid because of his job. They even buy their car, but because of the pandemic, the car was foreclosed. Also, some of Peter's things in shoot and editing have been sold because both of them have no work. His co-worker in video editing tells the kindness story of Peter in charity. Because of that the owner of the charity instantly gave him 30,000 cash and Peter built their Milk tea house. Now, they have 6 branches, 4 franchises and the rest is history.
